Visual Artist

My name is Valerie Ogorodnyk. I am a visual artist and photographer.I was born in Simferopol, Crimea, Ukraine 1987. My first art teacher taught me the basics of watercolour and drawing and opened my art soul, Ivan Shevchenko, famous Crimean painter.I was painting and drawing from 11 years old, having private art classes and visiting art galleries, as a hobby.
