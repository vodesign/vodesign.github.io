Beauty in the eye of the beholder.

My practice has dealt with the theme of women's beauty, the aging process and constructed ideal of the female image in contemporary society. With my work I confront the subject of commoditization and idealization of the female body within the fashion industry and consumer culture. My goal is to createimages that challenge the commercialized cult of body and explore the perception of beauty.
